Pitch: give them something to remember you by.

When you meet a new person on the job hunt, the elevator pitch allows you to convey your value proposition.

Behaviorial interview

During the interview process in the United States, the job candidate is often asked a series of behavior question.

  • Tell me about how you worked effectively under pressure.
  • How do you handle a challenge? Give an example.
  • Have you ever made a mistake? How did you handle it?
The PAR format is a good way to answer these questions. PAR stands for problem, action and results. Consider the queston about overcoming a challenge. You'd need to tell the interviewer about a specific problem in your career where you faced a challenge. Outline the actions you told to overcome it. Finally, you should talk about results.
