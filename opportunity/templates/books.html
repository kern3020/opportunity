What Color Is Your Parachute? is a classic job search book. It helps you find the kind of work about which you are passionate, take stock of your skills and actively pursue it.
The Start-up of You is a new book by Reid Hoffman, the CEO of LinkedIn, and Ben Casnocha. They apply key concepts from the startup world to the process of hunting for a job. These authors believe there are there things you need: assets(skills), aspiration(passion) and market reality(someone will to pay for it).
